## Common Questions

### Q: How often is data updated?
A: Click "Refresh" to get the latest data. Most indicators update daily.

### Q: What does "Strengthening" mean?
A: The correlation between two indicators is getting stronger vs. the prior period.

### Q: Why is Simple Mode different from Pro?
A: Simple Mode translates institutional analysis into beginner-friendly language. The conclusion is the same; only the presentation differs.
